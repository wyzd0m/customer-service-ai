# Warranty Policy

## General Coverage
All Northstar Outfitters gear includes a 1-year manufacturer's warranty
against defects in materials and workmanship from the date of purchase.

## Category-Specific Coverage
- **Footwear:** 1-year warranty on soles and stitching
- **Outerwear (jackets, rain shells):** 2-year warranty on waterproofing and seam taping
- **Hardgoods (tents, packs, hydration gear):** 1-year warranty on hardware, zippers, and frame components

## What Is Not Covered
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., worn-down soles, faded fabric color over time)
- Damage from misuse, improper storage, or lack of basic care
- Items purchased from unauthorized third-party resellers

## How to File a Warranty Claim
1. Contact support with the order number (or proof of purchase) and photos showing the defect
2. The support team will confirm whether the issue is covered under warranty
3. Approved claims are repaired, replaced, or refunded at Northstar Outfitters' discretion

Warranty claims involving a specific order should be escalated to a human
support agent, since they require reviewing the customer's purchase history.
