# Escalation and Contact Rules

## When to Escalate to a Human Agent
A question should be escalated instead of answered directly when it involves:
- A specific customer's order, account, or payment details that can't be verified here
- A complaint, dispute, or request for compensation beyond standard policy
- A request to cancel or change an order outside the 1-hour self-service window
- A question with no clear answer in the knowledge base
- Anything related to account security (password resets, suspicious activity, etc.)

## How Customers Can Reach a Human Agent
- Email: support@northstaroutfitters.example
- Live chat: available on the website, Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm ET
- Phone: 1-800-555-0182, Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm ET

## What to Have Ready
Customers should have their order number and the email address used at
checkout ready when contacting a human agent — this speeds up lookup time.

## Tone for Escalation Messages
Escalation messages should be brief, polite, and not apologize excessively.
Clearly state that a human agent is needed and how to reach one.
