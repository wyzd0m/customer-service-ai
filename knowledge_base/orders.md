# Order Tracking and Order Changes

## Tracking an Order
- A tracking link is emailed automatically once an order ships
- Orders can also be tracked from the "Order Status" page on the website
  using the order number and the email address used at checkout

## Typical Timeline
- Orders ship within 1-2 business days of being placed
- Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to update after a shipping label is created

## Tracking Not Updating
If tracking has not updated in 5 or more business days, this should be
escalated to a human support agent so they can contact the carrier directly.

## Changing or Cancelling an Order
Orders can only be changed or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, before
they enter processing. After that window, the order cannot be modified through
self-service and the request should be escalated to a human support agent,
who can check whether the order has already shipped.
