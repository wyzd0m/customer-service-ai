# Product Troubleshooting

## Headlamps and Flashlights Won't Turn On
1. Check that batteries are inserted in the correct orientation
2. Try a fresh set of batteries
3. Check battery contacts for corrosion and clean gently with a dry cloth if needed
4. Reset the light by holding the power button for 10 seconds

## Water Bottles Leaking
1. Make sure the lid is fully and evenly threaded, not cross-threaded
2. Inspect the rubber gasket inside the lid for cracks or wear
3. Worn gaskets can be replaced — contact support for a replacement part

## Tents Leaking
1. Reapply seam sealer to interior seams, especially around the floor and rainfly
2. Make sure the rainfly is staked out tight and not sagging or touching the tent body
3. Avoid touching the tent walls from the inside during heavy rain, as this can
   cause water to wick through

## Backpack Zippers Stuck or Hard to Pull
1. Check the zipper track for dirt, sand, or debris and clear it gently
2. Apply a small amount of paraffin wax or a silicone-based zipper lubricant
3. Avoid forcing a stuck zipper, as this can damage the slider or teeth

## Boots Leaking
1. Reapply a waterproofing treatment (recommended every 3-6 months with regular use)
2. Check for visible cracks, tears, or separation in the upper material
3. If the boots are still leaking after re-treatment and are within the warranty
   period, this should be escalated as a possible warranty claim
