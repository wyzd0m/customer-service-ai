# Shipping Policy

## Processing Time
Orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days of being placed.

## Standard Shipping
- 5-7 business days within the continental US
- Free on orders over $75
- $6.99 flat rate on orders under $75

## Expedited Shipping
- 2-3 business days within the continental US
- $14.99 flat rate

## International Shipping
- Available to most countries
- 10-21 business days depending on destination
- Shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
- Customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties charged by their country

## Order Confirmation and Tracking
A confirmation email is sent immediately after checkout. A separate email with
a tracking link is sent once the order ships.
